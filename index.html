About Us

Seng Computer Services, a computer service provider founded on March 2013. We've founded SCS to provide computer services for local students, typically UTAR and KTAR students to solve their computer problems.

As years goes on, we've expended our coverage area to Ipoh and added in more services like mobile repair and web development service for our customers.

As of today, we've been serving over thousands of customers around the area. Our mission is to provide honest, fast, reasonable price and best experience to our customers.
